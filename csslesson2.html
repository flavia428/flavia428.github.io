what is a border property?

a property that allows you to style around the border and indicates the size of an element

what are some of the different border properties?

there are 5 different border properties, such as borders, borders width, borders colour, border sides, and rounded borders

what is the difference between margins and padding?

margins is the spacing around an element from everything around it and padding is the spacing content has from the element border
